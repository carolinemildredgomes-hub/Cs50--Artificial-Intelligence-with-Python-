# Analysis

## Layer 6, Head 3

This attention head appears to focus on the relationship between determiners and the nouns that follow them. Words such as "the", "a", and "an" consistently attend strongly to nearby nouns.

Example Sentences:

- The cat slept on the chair.
- A teacher entered the classroom.

---

## Layer 8, Head 5

This attention head appears to capture relationships between pronouns and the nouns they refer to. Pronouns often attend strongly to the corresponding person or object mentioned earlier in the sentence.

Example Sentences:

- Alice smiled because she won.
- John forgot his wallet before leaving.
